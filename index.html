About

What is Tech Battle?

Acropolis Institute of Technology & Research has set up a program that aims to foster logical thinking, problem-solving skills, and coding abilities among its students. The purpose of TechBattle is to improve the thinking ability of budding coders and Competitive Programming Enthusiasts, by providing a new flavor of questions that will challenge them to get better at coding.

Who is eligible to take part in Tech Battle?

The Tech Battle contest is open to all Acropolis Institute students of first, second, and third year.

The competition's logistics are as follows:

1. There will be two contests per month, with a final event planned at the end of the year. The points earned in the contest that is held each month will be used to determine the winning entry. 2. Each contest will span 48 hours (two days), starting on Friday at 10:00 p.m. and ending on Sunday at 10:00 PM. 3. Contest will be held on Hacker Rank. 4. Contest link will be provided at https://sites.google.com/view/aitrtechbattle/home. 5. Students are required to register for the contest using their Hacker Rank UserId. Registration and participation in contest are free. Only registered students will be considered valid. Registration form link : https://forms.gle/X4BVQVKAGAGC4Nsm9 6. Students must score a minimum of 40% to qualify for the rewards 7. Top three winners from each year of study entitled cash prize of Rs. 500, Rs. 300, and Rs. 200 respectively. 8. Students, who are in winner list for three contests consecutively, will not be considered again for cash prizes, but they can continue participation to earn points to be shortlisted for final contest. 9. Students are advised to put their original solution and restrain themselves from any unethical practices. Students found in any unlawful act, will not be eligible for any test then onwards.
